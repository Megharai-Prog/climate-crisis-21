Climate Change Facts

Rising Global Temperatures

The Earth's average temperature has risen by 1.1°C since the late 19th century, with most warming occurring in the past 40 years. The last decade (2011-2020) was the warmest on record.

Learn more

Deforestation Crisis

Approximately 10 million hectares of forest are destroyed each year, equivalent to 27 soccer fields every minute. This accounts for 12-20% of global greenhouse gas emissions.

Learn more

Renewable Energy Growth

In 2022, renewable energy sources accounted for 30% of global electricity generation. Solar energy costs have dropped 89% in the last decade, making it the cheapest electricity in history.

Learn more

Plastic Pollution

Humans produce about 400 million tons of plastic waste annually. Only 9% of all plastic waste ever produced has been recycled, with 79% accumulating in landfills or the natural environment.

Learn more

AI's Energy Demand

Training a single AI model can emit as much carbon as 5 cars in their lifetimes. Data centers (for AI and other tech) consume about 1% of global electricity, projected to double by 2026.

Learn more

Climate Migration

Climate change could force over 140 million people to migrate within their countries by 2050. Small island nations face existential threats from rising sea levels, with some possibly disappearing.

Learn more

BREAKING NEWS

Last week, the International Consortium of Investigative Journalists unveiled shocking new data about corporate climate pledges. Despite public commitments, 80% of major corporations are on track to miss their 2030 emission reduction targets.
